About me

This little corner of the web belongs to Eric Huffman, a front-end software engineer, and occasional illustrator. For my full professional history see my LinkedIn profile.

I'm a native Hoosier, but have hung my hat in Oregon for the past 5+ years now after spending the majority of my adult life in Atlanta.

These days I make my living as part of the team at CivicActions where we're helping transform digital services for the federal government.

Outside of day job work I often stoke the inner fires of my fine art roots. I hope to soon start showcasing those efforts on this site (#staytuned).
